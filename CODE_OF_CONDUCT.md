# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Being respectful and constructive in feedback
- Accepting differing opinions and approaches
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other contributors

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive conduct
- Any other conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer directly.  
The maintainer reserves the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
